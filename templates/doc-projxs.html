ProjXs Documentation

Welcome to ProjXs

ProjXs is your ultimate productivity companion, designed to help you manage your projects, tasks, and time effectively. Created by PyGen Labs under the leadership of Ameer Hamza Khan (Founder and CEO), ProjXs is built with the user in mind, offering a seamless and intuitive experience for all your project management needs.

Key Features

  • Add Notes: Quickly jot down ideas, tasks, or any important information related to your projects.
  • Project Management: Organize your work into projects, keeping everything neatly structured and easily accessible.
  • Tagging System: Categorize and filter your notes and projects with customizable tags for efficient organization.
  • Real-time Calculations: Stay on top of your progress with instant updates and calculations as you work.
  • Productivity Champion: Compete with yourself and others to earn the title of Productivity Champion, awarded to the most active and consistent users.

Getting Started

To begin your productivity journey with ProjXs:

  1. Sign up for an account on our website or mobile app.
  2. Create your first project by clicking the "New Project" button.
  3. Add notes to your project and tag them for easy reference.
  4. Track your progress and watch your productivity soar!
